Started off as an experiment: 1-man job, single-node Redshift cluster, a bunch of tables for some big joins...

Redcat is now expanding into a 6-node cluster, taking in data all over the place, running all different type of massive analytics, becoming the one and only Data Warehouse for Zalora, across all ventures.

I can't say I'm not proud of what I've done :).
